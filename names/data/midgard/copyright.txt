Midgard is a Pen&Paper Role Playing Game from Germany

The countries there are basing on interesting cultures from the real earth with a few modifications (magic, especially).

Cultures are alba (scottish), eschar (oriental), küstenstaaten (a bit of italy the machiavelli-time) and so on.

more can be found at
http://www.midgard-online.de
